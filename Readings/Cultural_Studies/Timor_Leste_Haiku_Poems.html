Timor-Leste Haiku Series: Tetum and English

Matenek Foho nian / Mountain Wisdom
Tetum:
Tutun Ramelau murmura
Lia loos antigu iha dader nia abuabu
Hanoin nia ilusaun lakon
English:
Ramelau's peak whispers
Ancient truths in morning mist
Mind's illusions fade
Tasi nia Konsiénsia / Ocean of Consciousness
Tetum:
Tasi ibun Atauro dere
Ho moris nia sa'e no tun - kontente
Terus dansa iha tasi furin
English:
Atauro's shores pulse
With life's ebb and flow - pleasure
Pain dance in sea foam
Lafaek Lulik / Sacred Crocodile
Tetum:
Abo lafaek dolar
Liu ai parapa - sasin nonook
Ba moris nia unidade
English:
Grandfather croc glides
Through mangroves - silent witness
To life's unity
Padraun Tais nian / Tais Patterns
Tetum:
Kabas tais soru hamutuk
Hanesan hanoin - husik hela, depois haree
Padraun tomak nabilan
English:
Tais threads interweave
Like thoughts - let them be, then watch
The whole pattern shine
Meditasaun Kafé / Coffee Meditation
Tetum:
Iha to'os Ermera
Musan kafé ida-idak kaer sabór
Eternidade nian
English:
In Ermera's fields
Each coffee bean holds the taste
Of eternity
Murmura Fataluku / Fataluku Whispers
Tetum:
Anin Fataluku kanta
Kona-ba la-dualidade - rona
Liu hosi lian
English:
Fataluku winds sing
Of non-duality - hear
Beyond the language
Matenek Ira Lafai nian / Ira Lafai Wisdom
Tetum:
Ira Lafai lulik
Proteje buka-door sira - iha nia mahon
Hetan hun ne'ebé la iha hun
English:
Sacred Ira Lafai
Shelters seekers - in its shade
Find the sourceless source
Hanorin Tasi Timor nian / Timor Sea Teachings
Tetum:
Tasi Timor refleta
Lalehan - hanesan hanoin refleta lia loos
Mamuk no nakonu
English:
Timor Sea reflects
The sky - like mind mirrors truth
Both empty and full
Dader iha Dili / Dili's Dawn
Tetum:
Dili hadeer - merkadu
Lian mosu hosi nonook, hatudu
Dansa moris nian
English:
Dili wakes - market
Sounds arise from silence, show
The dance of being
Cristo Rei nia Fitun / Cristo Rei's Gaze
Tetum:
Cristo Rei hamriik aas
Abrasa hotu - iha nia vizaun
La iha separasaun
English:
Cristo Rei stands tall
Embracing all - in his view
No separation
Mehi Ilha Jaco nian / Jaco Island Dreams
Tetum:
Iha tasi ibun moos Jaco
Ain fatin mosu, depois lakon
Temporáriu koalia
English:
On Jaco's pure shores
Footprints appear, then vanish
Impermanence speaks
Nino Konis nia Liman Rohan / Nino Konis' Legacy
Tetum:
Nino Konis nia mehi
Kona-ba liberdade halo ekos bolu
Atu liberta hanoin
English:
Nino Konis' dream
Of freedom echoes the call
To liberate mind
Abuabu Aileu nian / Aileu's Mists
Tetum:
Abuabu Aileu hatudu
Depois taka foho - hanesan hanoin
Sa'e, monu, lakon
English:
Aileu's mists reveal
Then conceal the hills - like thoughts
Rising, falling, gone
Klean Atauro nian / Atauro Depths
Tetum:
Mout ba Atauro nia klean
Jardin ahu ruin refleta hanoin nia
Padraun infinitu
English:
Dive Atauro's depths
Coral gardens mirror mind's
Infinite patterns
Loron Sa'e iha Los Palos / Los Palos Sunrise
Tetum:
Los Palos kumumprimentu dader
Loron foun, an foun mosu
Agora nafatin
English:
Los Palos greets dawn
New day, new self emerges
Perpetual now
Segredu Uma Lulik nian / Uma Lulik Secrets
Tetum:
Uma Lulik hamriik
Guardiaun ba mistériu sira
Tama ba o nia fuan laran
English:
Uma Lulik stands
Guardian of mysteries
Enter your own heart
