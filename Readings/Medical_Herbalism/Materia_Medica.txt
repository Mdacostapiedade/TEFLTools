Yarrow (Achillea millefolium)
Parts Used: Aerial parts
Key constituents: Volatile oil, sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, alkaloids, tannins
Key actions and indications:
- Diaphoretic: Helps body deal with fever
- Hypotensive: Lowers blood pressure through dilation of peripheral vessels
- Astringent: Tones blood vessels 
- Anti-inflammatory
- Antispasmodic
- Diuretic
- Antimicrobial
- Bitter
- Hepatic
Specific uses: Fever, hypertension, thrombotic conditions, wound healing, upper respiratory infections
Preparations:
- Tincture: 2-4 ml three times daily (1:5 in 25% alcohol)
- Infusion: 1-2 tsp dried herb per cup, steep 10-15 minutes. Drink 3x daily or hourly for fever.
Cautions: Possible hypersensitivity. Avoid during pregnancy.

Horse Chestnut (Aesculus hippocastanum)  
Parts Used: Seeds, pericarp
Key constituents: Triterpene saponin glycosides (aescin), flavonoids, coumarin derivatives
Key actions and indications:
- Astringent 
- Anti-inflammatory
- Venous tonic: Increases elasticity and tone of veins, decreases vein permeability
Specific uses: Varicose veins, hemorrhoids, leg ulcers, chronic venous insufficiency
Preparations:
- Standardized extract: 50 mg aescin 2-3 times daily
- Tincture: 1-4 ml three times daily (1:5 in 40% alcohol)
- Infusion: 1-2 tsp dried fruit per cup, steep 10-15 minutes. Drink 3x daily.
Cautions: May interact with anticoagulant medications due to coumarin content.

Key actions and indications:
- Alterative
- Diuretic
- Laxative
- Cardiac stimulant
Specific uses: Skin conditions (eczema, psoriasis), constipation
Preparations:
- Tincture: 2-4 ml three times daily (1:5 in 40% alcohol)
- Infusion: 1-3 tsp dried leaf per cup, steep 10-15 minutes. Drink 3x daily.
Cautions: May potentiate effects of cardiac glycosides.

Skullcap (Scutellaria lateriflora)
Parts Used: Aerial parts
Key constituents: Flavonoids, iridoids, volatile oil, tannins
Key actions and indications:
- Nervine tonic
- Antispasmodic
- Hypotensive
Specific uses: Anxiety, insomnia, nervous tension, premenstrual tension
Preparations:
- Tincture: 2-4 ml three times daily (1:5 in 40% alcohol)
- Infusion: 1-2 tsp dried herb per cup, steep 10-15 minutes. Drink 3x daily or as needed.
Cautions: Can potentiate effects of sedative medications.

Senna (Senna alexandrina)
Parts Used: Dried fruit pod, leaf
Key constituents: Anthraquinone glycosides, naphthalene glycosides
Action: Cathartic
Specific uses: Constipation
Preparations:
- Infusion: Steep dried pods/leaves in warm water for 6-12 hours. Drink in morning and at night.
Cautions: May reduce absorption of oral drugs. Can cause potassium loss. Avoid in pregnancy, lactation, and children under 12. Do not use for more than 10 days.

Saw Palmetto (Serenoa repens)
Part Used: Fruit (berry)
Key constituents: Essential oil, fixed oil, sterols, polysaccharides
Key actions and indications:
- Diuretic
- Urinary antiseptic
- Endocrine agent
Specific uses: Benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH), urinary difficulties
Preparations:
- Tincture: 1-2 ml three times daily (1:5 in 60% alcohol)
- Decoction: 2-4 tsp berries per cup, simmer 5 minutes. Drink 3x daily.
- Standardized extract: 320 mg daily of lipophilic extract
Cautions: No known side effects or drug interactions reported.

Milk Thistle (Silybum marianum)
Part Used: Seed
Key constituents: Flavolignans (silymarin), fixed oil, sterols
Key actions and indications:
- Hepatic
- Galactagogue
- Demulcent
- Cholagogue
- Antihepatotoxic
Specific uses: Liver protection and support, hepatitis, cirrhosis, gallbladder conditions
Preparations:
- Standardized extract: 175 mg daily of 30:1 extract (80% silymarin) for protection, up to 600 mg daily for therapeutic effects
Cautions: No known side effects or drug interactions reported.

Sarsaparilla (Smilax spp.)
Parts Used: Root, rhizome
Key constituents: Saponins, sterols
Key actions and indications:
- Alterative
- Antirheumatic
- Diaphoretic
- Diuretic
Specific uses: Skin conditions (psoriasis), rheumatism, rheumatoid arthritis
Preparations:
- Tincture: 1-2 ml three times daily (1:5 in 60% alcohol)
- Decoction: 2-4 tsp root per cup, simmer 10-15 minutes. Drink 3x daily.
Cautions: May increase absorption of digitalis glycosides.
