# Engaging Activities for Introduction to Desalination and Water Challenges in Timor-Leste

## Warm-up Activities

### 1. Water Usage Estimation
- Students estimate their daily water usage
- Compare estimates with actual average usage in Timor-Leste
- Discuss differences and implications

### 2. Water Source Mapping
- Students draw a quick map of their community's water sources
- Share and compare maps with classmates
- Identify common water sources and potential challenges

## Main Lesson Activities

### 1. Desalination Demonstration
- Use a simple salt water solution and coffee filter to demonstrate basic filtration
- Explain how this relates to more complex desalination processes
- Discuss limitations of this simple method compared to advanced technologies

### 2. Virtual Tour of Desalination Plant
- Show a virtual tour video of a desalination plant
- Students take notes on key components and processes
- Follow-up with a Q&A session to clarify understanding

## Group Work or Pair Work Tasks

### 1. Water Challenge Brainstorming
- In small groups, brainstorm water challenges specific to Timor-Leste
- Create a mind map connecting challenges to potential solutions
- Present findings to the class

### 2. Desalination Pros and Cons Debate
- Divide class into pro-desalination and anti-desalination groups
- Groups prepare arguments for their assigned position
- Conduct a structured debate on the merits of desalination for Timor-Leste

## Individual Practice Exercises

### 1. Vocabulary Crossword Puzzle
- Create a crossword puzzle using key vocabulary terms
- Students complete the puzzle individually
- Review answers as a class

### 2. Water Resource Infographic
- Students create an infographic about Timor-Leste's water resources
- Include data on water availability, quality, and distribution
- Share infographics in a gallery walk format

## Cool-down or Wrap-up Activities

### 1. 3-2-1 Reflection
- Students write down:
  * 3 things they learned
  * 2 questions they still have
  * 1 way the lesson connects to their life
- Share reflections with a partner or small group

### 2. Water Conservation Pledge
- Students write a personal pledge to conserve water
- Include specific actions they will take in their daily lives
- Volunteers share their pledges with the class
