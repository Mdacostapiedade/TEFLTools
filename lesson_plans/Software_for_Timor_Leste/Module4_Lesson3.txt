# Lesson Plan: Introduction to Cryptocurrency Concepts

## Duration: 1 day (3 hours)

### Objectives:
- Understand the basics of cryptocurrency and blockchain technology
- Learn about Bitcoin and other major cryptocurrencies
- Grasp the concept of decentralization and its implications
- Understand basic cryptographic principles used in cryptocurrencies

### Materials:
- Computers with internet access
- Whiteboard and markers
- Handouts on cryptocurrency basics

### Session Outline:

1. Introduction to Cryptocurrency (45 minutes)
   a. Definition and brief history
   b. How cryptocurrencies differ from traditional currencies
   c. The concept of digital scarcity
   d. Overview of major cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.)

2. Blockchain Technology (45 minutes)
   a. What is a blockchain?
   b. How blockchain enables cryptocurrencies
   c. Decentralization and its benefits
   d. Consensus mechanisms (Proof of Work, Proof of Stake)

3. Break (15 minutes)

4. Cryptography in Cryptocurrencies (30 minutes)
   a. Public and private keys
   b. Digital signatures
   c. Hashing functions

5. Practical Aspects of Cryptocurrencies (30 minutes)
   a. Wallets and addresses
   b. Transactions and fees
   c. Mining and block rewards
   d. Exchanges and trading

6. Discussion and Q&A (15 minutes)
   - Potential impacts of cryptocurrencies on Timor-Leste's economy
   - Challenges and opportunities in adopting cryptocurrencies

### Homework:
Research and write a short report on how cryptocurrency adoption might affect remittances to Timor-Leste.

### Assessment:
- Class participation
- Short quiz on basic cryptocurrency concepts
- Homework assignment

### Additional Resources:
- "Mastering Bitcoin" by Andreas M. Antonopoulos
- CoinDesk's cryptocurrency explainers
- Blockchain.com's learning resources
