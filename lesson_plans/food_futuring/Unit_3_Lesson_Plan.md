# Lesson Plan: Cultural Identity and Food Heritage in Timor-Leste

## 1. Resources Needed

- Whiteboard and markers
- Projector and computer
- Printed handouts of traditional Timorese recipes
- Audio recording equipment for interviews
- Traditional Timorese cooking utensils (if available)
- Ingredients for a simple traditional Timorese dish

## 2. Lesson Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:
- Explain the significance of food in Timorese cultural identity
- Describe at least three traditional Timorese food rituals or ceremonies
- Identify challenges in preserving Timorese food heritage
- Conduct and analyze an interview about traditional food knowledge

## 3. Warm-up Activity (10 minutes)

- Students share a food from their own culture that holds special significance
- Brief discussion on how food connects to cultural identity

## 4. Pre-teaching Key Vocabulary (10 minutes)

Introduce and explain key terms:
- Food heritage
- Cultural identity
- Traditional knowledge
- Food rituals
- Culinary preservation

## 5. Presentation of Main Lesson Content (30 minutes)

### a. Food Rituals and Ceremonies in Timorese Culture
- Discuss the role of food in life cycle events (birth, marriage, death)
- Explain seasonal food rituals and their connection to agriculture
- Present examples of communal eating practices

### b. Traditional Knowledge of Food Production and Preparation
- Overview of traditional farming techniques
- Explanation of traditional food preservation methods
- Demonstration of a traditional cooking technique

### c. Challenges to Maintaining Food Heritage
- Discuss the impact of globalization on food choices
- Explain the loss of traditional knowledge across generations
- Present economic factors affecting traditional food practices

## 6. Practice Activities (40 minutes)

### a. Group Analysis of Traditional Recipes
- Students work in small groups to analyze traditional Timorese recipes
- Identify ingredients, techniques, and cultural significance

### b. Role-play: Food Ritual Scenario
- Groups create and perform a short role-play of a Timorese food ritual

## 7. Production Tasks (60 minutes)

### a. Interview Preparation
- Students prepare questions for elder interviews about traditional food knowledge
- Practice interview techniques and active listening skills

### b. Mock Interviews
- Students conduct mock interviews with classmates playing the role of elders
- Feedback provided on interview techniques and question quality

## 8. Wrap-up and Review (15 minutes)

- Class discussion on key learnings about Timorese food heritage
- Quick quiz on main concepts covered in the lesson

## 9. Homework Assignment

- Conduct an actual interview with a Timorese elder about traditional food knowledge
- Prepare a 5-minute presentation on a specific Timorese food tradition for next class

## 10. Key Vocabulary Definitions

- Food heritage: The cultural traditions, practices, and knowledge related to food production, preparation, and consumption passed down through generations.
- Cultural identity: The characteristics, beliefs, and practices that define a particular group of people.
- Traditional knowledge: Information, skills, and practices developed and maintained by a community over time.
- Food rituals: Ceremonial practices involving food that hold cultural or religious significance.
- Culinary preservation: The act of maintaining and protecting traditional cooking methods, recipes, and food-related practices.
