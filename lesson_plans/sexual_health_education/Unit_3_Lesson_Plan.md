# Lesson Plan: Sexual and Reproductive Health

## 1. Resources Needed

- Contraceptive demonstration kit
- Condoms and lubricant
- Anatomical models of male and female reproductive systems
- Posters or handouts on STI symptoms and prevention
- Pregnancy and prenatal care information sheets
- Whiteboard and markers
- Anonymous question box

## 2. Lesson Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:
- Identify and explain at least 3 contraceptive methods available in Timor-Leste
- Demonstrate correct condom use
- List common STIs and their prevention methods
- Describe basic prenatal care practices

## 3. Warm-up Activity (10 minutes)

True/False quiz on contraception and STI myths. Students stand on one side of the room for "True" and the other for "False". Discuss correct answers as a group.

## 4. Pre-teaching Key Vocabulary (10 minutes)

Introduce and explain key terms:
- Contraception
- Sexually Transmitted Infection (STI)
- Prenatal care
- Condom
- Birth control pill
- Intrauterine Device (IUD)

## 5. Main Lesson Content (40 minutes)

### Contraceptive Methods (20 minutes)
- Present overview of contraceptive methods available in Timor-Leste
- Explain how each method works, effectiveness, and considerations
- Use contraceptive kit for visual demonstrations

### STI Prevention (10 minutes)
- Discuss common STIs in Timor-Leste
- Explain transmission, symptoms, and prevention methods
- Emphasize importance of testing and treatment

### Pregnancy and Prenatal Care (10 minutes)
- Overview of pregnancy signs and symptoms
- Discuss importance of prenatal care
- Explain basic prenatal care practices

## 6. Practice Activities (30 minutes)

### Condom Demonstration (15 minutes)
- Demonstrate correct condom use on anatomical model
- Have students practice in pairs using bananas or wooden models

### STI Prevention Matching Game (15 minutes)
- In small groups, match STI names with symptoms and prevention methods

## 7. Production Task (20 minutes)

Create informational posters on either:
1. Steps for correct condom use
2. STI prevention methods
3. Important prenatal care practices

## 8. Wrap-up and Review (10 minutes)

- Quick quiz on main points covered
- Address any questions from anonymous question box

## 9. Homework Assignment

Write a short essay (1-2 pages) on the importance of contraception OR prenatal care in Timor-Leste. Include challenges and potential solutions.

## 10. Key Vocabulary Definitions

- Contraception: Methods used to prevent pregnancy
- Sexually Transmitted Infection (STI): Infections spread through sexual contact
- Prenatal care: Healthcare provided during pregnancy
- Condom: Barrier contraceptive method that also prevents STIs
- Birth control pill: Oral contraceptive taken daily to prevent pregnancy
- Intrauterine Device (IUD): Long-acting reversible contraceptive inserted into the uterus
