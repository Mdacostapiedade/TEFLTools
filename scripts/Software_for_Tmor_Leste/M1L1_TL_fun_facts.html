Cuisine of Timor-Leste

The cuisine of Timor-Leste reflects the country's diverse cultural influences, including Portuguese, Indonesian, and indigenous Timorese traditions. Rice, cassava, and corn are staple foods, often served with fish, pork, or chicken.

Popular Dishes

Dish Description
Batar Da'an A traditional Timorese dish made with corn, pumpkin, and beans.
Ikan Pepes Fish cooked in banana leaves with spices and coconut milk.
Tapai A fermented cassava or rice dish, often served as a dessert.
